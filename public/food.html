Food & Drinks

The Bias family shortlist — tried, tested, and genuinely loved.

Bennie's Red Barn
This is where my parents had their first date. A St. Simons steakhouse institution — rustic, warm, and exactly what a Southern steakhouse should be. Non-negotiable for our family.
Southern Soul Barbecue
Featured on a national TV BBQ show. Slow-smoked, no-frills, the real thing. Near Redfern Village. If you've never been, you go this trip.
Sweet Mama's
Best breakfast and brunch on the island. Get there early — lines form for a reason. Plan a slow morning around it.
The Crab Trap
Fresh local seafood, good views, laid-back atmosphere. A St. Simons institution for a reason. Come hungry.
Brogen's
Right at the Pier. Cold drinks, good food, live music most nights. This is where the post-beach crowd lands. Great vibe.
Wolf Island Oyster Company
Local oysters and coastal fare done simply and well. Named after one of the Golden Isles' most pristine wildlife refuges. The food matches the setting.

"Nightly live music at the Pier and Redfern Village. Ghost tours after dark. Putt-putt. Sunset walks. The evenings here are as good as the days."
