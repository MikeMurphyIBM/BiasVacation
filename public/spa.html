Spa & Shops

The Cloister set the standard in 1928. It still holds it.

"The Spa at Sea Island is 65,000 square feet of Forbes Five-Star. It's been rated one of the best resort spas in the country for decades. If you're going to splurge once on this trip, this is the one."

The Cloister Spa — Sea Island
Forbes Five-Star. 65,000 sq ft. Massages, facials, body treatments, sound journeys, a labyrinth walk. Requires a stay at The Cloister or The Lodge, or a day reservation as a non-guest.
The King and Prince Spa
St. Simons' own option — oceanfront, full treatment menu. A great choice if you want the spa experience without crossing to Sea Island.
Pier Village & Mallery Street
The waterfront shopping district. Local boutiques, galleries, bait shops, and gifts — all walkable from the Pier and Lighthouse. Best experienced on an evening stroll.
Redfern Village
Tree-lined streets, locally owned boutiques, art galleries. Southern Soul BBQ is right here, so your shopping trip has a mandatory pit stop built in.
