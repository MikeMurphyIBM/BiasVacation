East Beach, St. Simons Island

Where to Stay

Near the Coast Guard Station. Pool preferred. Bike to everything.

"East Beach is the sweet spot — central to the grocery store, the beach, the pier, and the best restaurants. The homes here are the nicest on the island, most with pools. Very little traffic."

East Beach Vacation Rentals
Look near the Coast Guard Station for the best homes. Private pools are common. Search VRBO or Airbnb and filter East Beach or "East End." Book early — summer fills fast.
Location Is Everything
10-minute bike ride to the Pier. Walk to East Beach. Grocery store is close. No car needed for most of your trip — which is the whole idea.
The King and Prince Resort
The island's historic oceanfront hotel. Has its own spa, pools, and a restaurant with views. A solid option if you'd rather not manage a rental house.
The Beach Itself
Wide, flat, and uncrowded. You won't be shoulder-to-shoulder with strangers. The sandbar at low tide is a bonus. Sunrises here are worth setting an alarm for.
